About Us

Techspardha(formerly Literati) is the annual Techno-Managerial festival of National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra. Established in the year 1995 as Technospect with 9 events, the extravaganza transformed into Literati the following year attracting students from in and around Kurukshetra. The last edition, Literati 2012 attracted participation from 200 colleges for on campus events and over 300 colleges across the globe competed online for the supremacy. The year 2012 marks the completion of 50 years of Engineering Excellence of NIT Kurukshetra and thus the mega technical event from this year would be re-christened as TECHSPARDHA with the motive "Igniting Innovation".
    Mission
  • To strive to achieve technical advancement in resonance with the sustainable practices.
  • To encourage students in exploring new frontiers in technical as well as management .
  • To provide an opportunity to the budding engineers, scientists, technocrats, managers and entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas and mould them into concrete form so as to change the face of tomorrow.
Over 65 events from all the 7 branches of the college, 10,000 participants and 50,000 people from all over the country, its going to be historic. With massive cash prizes and a feeling like never before, TECHSPARDHA awaits your presence as a participant, spectator and a witness.
